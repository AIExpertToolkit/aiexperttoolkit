Pricing & Limits — FAQs

Annual vs. monthly?

Annual typically saves ~20% vs. month-to-month. Pay upfront; switch tiers anytime. Refund policy is usually 7 days.

Can I add more users/projects?

Yes. Standard: +1 user at $40; Pro: +1 at $50; Advanced: +up to 5 at $60 each. Extra projects: $49/$99/$149 respectively.

Do credits/articles roll over?

No — quotas reset each billing cycle. Plan your content calendar to use them.

What’s a “project”?

A unique domain/website. You’ll need separate projects to run audits & tracking per domain.
