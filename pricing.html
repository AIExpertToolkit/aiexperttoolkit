Pricing & Limits — FAQs

Are there usage caps?

Yes. Starter is best for light use; Pro increases throughput; Business is best for sustained volume. Always check the checkout page for current specifics.

Can I change plans later?

Yes — upgrade or switch tiers as output and team needs grow.

Does the free option include long-form?

Expect limited access on Starter; Pro and Business unlock the full long-form experience.

Is BotSonic included?

It’s available on Business and sometimes as an add-on for Pro. Verify availability on the pricing page.
