Privacy Policy

Plain-English summary of what we collect and why.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage. IP addresses are anonymized. We do not sell personal data.

Affiliate links

Some outbound links are affiliate links. They never change your price. We add UTM parameters for attribution and reporting.

Emails

If you email us, we’ll use your message only to respond. We don’t share your email with third parties.
