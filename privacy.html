Your privacy

Privacy Policy

We collect as little data as possible to run this site. This page explains what, why, and your choices.

Last updated: August 14, 2025

What we collect

  • Basic logs from our host (GitHub Pages) like IP, user-agent, and requested URL for security and reliability.
  • Emails you send us (e.g., via Contact), stored in our email inbox.

What we don’t collect

  • No account sign-ups or passwords.
  • No payment info on our site.
  • No intrusive tracking pixels set by us.

Affiliate links & third parties

Some outbound links are affiliate links. If you purchase after clicking, the merchant may use cookies or similar tech to attribute the referral. Their privacy policy applies on their site.

Embedded content

When we embed third-party media (e.g., YouTube), those services may set cookies or collect data per their policies.

Your choices

  • Don’t submit personal information unless necessary.
  • Use your browser’s controls to block or clear cookies.
  • Contact us to request deletion of messages you’ve sent.

Data retention

Server logs rotate per our host’s defaults. Emails you send may be retained for record-keeping unless you request deletion.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us and we’ll help.
