Consulting Terms

Plain-English terms for the Build-With-You Website Sprint and related services.

Scope

The Sprint covers Jekyll + GitHub Pages setup/hardening, CWV & Accessibility improvements, SEO hygiene, and content scaffolds (comparison/review/pricing). Out-of-scope items include backends, custom apps, heavy redesigns, or large migrations unless separately agreed in writing.

Process & timeline

The Sprint is 10 business days from kickoff. Timely feedback from your decision-maker is required to stay on schedule. We’ll provide daily commits and brief Loom recordings where useful.

Fees & payment

  • Fixed fee (see Consulting page). 50% due at kickoff; 50% at launch. Invoicing via Stripe/ACH.
  • Any out-of-scope work is quoted separately and approved by you in writing before we proceed.

Delivery guarantee & refunds

  • If we do not launch within the 10-day window for reasons in our control, we continue at no additional cost until live and refund USD $500.
  • Delays caused by missing access, late feedback, new scope, or third-party outages extend the window accordingly.
  • You may cancel before Day 2 for a full refund of the kickoff payment.

Ownership & license

You own the resulting site code, SOPs, and assets upon full payment. We retain a non-exclusive right to reference non-confidential work outcomes in our portfolio unless you request otherwise in writing.

Confidentiality

Both parties will keep non-public information confidential and use it solely for the engagement.

Warranties & liability

Work is provided “as-is” with commercially reasonable care. We do not guarantee rankings or revenue. Our total liability is limited to the fees paid under this engagement.

Privacy

Consult bookings share only the data you provide. We do not sell or share personal information. If you maintain a Privacy Policy, it governs personal data you provide to us.

Acceptance

By paying an invoice or scheduling a kickoff, you agree to these terms. If you have procurement requirements, share them before kickoff.
