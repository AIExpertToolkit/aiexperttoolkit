5-step delivery checklist

  1. Collect info: product name, features, materials, size, audience, 2–3 competitor links, and brand voice sample.
  2. Draft in Writesonic: short title, 5 benefit-led bullets, 120–200-word description, 3 common FAQs.
  3. Make it human: read out loud, cut fluff, avoid big claims (esp. health/medical), keep sentences short.
  4. Quick SEO pass: main phrase in title + first sentence, naturally.
  5. Deliver clean: one Google Doc or CSV if they want upload-ready.
